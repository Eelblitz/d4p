Promotion playbook

The best promoted listings usually win before the boost even starts.

Fix the listing fundamentals first

Use strong cover images, a credible description, and a clean price story before you spend to amplify it.

Promote based on likely contact intent

Listings with clear demand and quick response workflows convert promotion much better than vague inventory.

Watch promoted CTR, not just views

More traffic only matters when contact clicks rise. DM4PRICE already shows whether promoted attention is becoming outreach.

Use short tests before big bets

FAQ

What sellers usually want to know before buying visibility.

How do I pay for a promotion?

During checkout, DM4PRICE initializes a Paystack payment and supports local methods such as bank transfer and USSD.

When does the promotion start?

The boost activates after Paystack confirms the transaction, then the plan duration begins automatically.

Can promoted products still be outranked?

Yes. Promotion helps, but the marketplace still values listing quality, seller verification, and relevance to the buyer query.

Where do I measure results?

Your seller dashboard shows promoted views, contact clicks, promoted CTR, and the top-performing listing signal.
